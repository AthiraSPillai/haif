# Jira Workflow

Jira should track committed execution, not every agent-generated possibility.

## Recommended Mapping

- HAIF `Proposal`: not a Jira task yet, or a Jira issue labeled `proposal`.
- HAIF `Proposal`: possible work or Jira candidate that is not committed yet.
- HAIF `Design`: proposed approach, API, architecture, rollout, or tradeoff document.
- HAIF `Decision`: human-approved direction that allows Jira execution work to proceed.
- Optional HAIF `Task`: execution work derived from an approved decision.
- HAIF `Conflict`: blocker or linked issue requiring review.

## Labels

- `agent-created`: generated by an AI agent.
- `needs-decision`: missing approved HAIF decision.
- `decision-linked`: linked to approved HAIF decision.
- `design-approved`: linked to approved design or decision.

## Policy

Agent-created Jira tickets should remain proposed until linked to an approved HAIF decision. Direct agent-created Jira tickets without an approved decision should be routed to review, not silently added to active backlog.
