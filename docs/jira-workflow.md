# Jira Workflow

Jira should track committed execution, not every agent-generated possibility.

## Recommended Mapping

- HAIF `Proposal`: not a Jira task yet, or a Jira issue labeled `proposal`.
- HAIF `Intent`: Jira epic, initiative, or story with owner and scope.
- HAIF `Decision`: linked design or architecture decision record.
- HAIF `Task`: Jira task, story, or subtask ready for execution.
- HAIF `Conflict`: blocker or linked issue requiring review.

## Labels

- `agent-created`: generated by an AI agent.
- `needs-intent`: missing accepted HAIF intent.
- `intent-linked`: linked to accepted intent.
- `design-approved`: linked to approved design or decision.

## Policy

Agent-created Jira tickets should remain proposed until linked to accepted HAIF intent. Direct agent-created Jira tickets without intent should be routed to review, not silently added to active backlog.
